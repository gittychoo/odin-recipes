Bolognese Recipe

Description

Meat sauced pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (or a combination of beef, pork, and/or veal)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 stalks celery, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, finely chopped
  • can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese and fresh parsley, for serving
  • 1 pound spaghetti or your preferred pasta

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, heat 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, celery, and carrots and cook until the vegetables are soft and the onion is translucent.
  2. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a wooden spoon as it cooks.
  3. Once the meat is browned, add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, beef broth, red wine, milk, and bay leaf to the skillet. Stir to combine.
  4. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and let it cook for at least 1 hour, stirring occasionally. You can let it cook for up to 3-4 hours for a more developed flavor.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Cook the spaghetti (or your preferred pasta) according to the package instructions. Drain the pasta and serve with the Bolognese sauce.
  7. Top each serving with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh parsley, if desired.
  8. Lastly, enjoy your delicious homemade Bolognese!
