Napoletana Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup San Marzano tomato sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 2-3 fresh basil leaves
  • 1-2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature possible (usually around 500-550°F or 260-290°C).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, stretch the pizza dough out into a circle, leaving the edges slightly thicker than the center.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza dough, leaving a 1/2 inch border around the edge.
  4. Arrange the sliced mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic, a drizzle of olive oil, and a pinch of salt. Spread this mixture over the pizza, avoiding the edges.
  6. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the crust is puffed and golden brown, and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two.
  8. Top the pizza with fresh basil leaves.
  9. Slice the pizza and serve immediately.
