Margherita Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. On a lightly oiled baking sheet or pizza stone, spread the pizza dough out into a circle.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza dough, leaving a 1/2 inch border around the edge.
  4. Arrange the sliced mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce.
  5. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two.
  7. Top the pizza with fresh basil leaves.
  8. Slice the pizza and serve immediately.
